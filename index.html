About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Peter Fernández I am a Programming Analyst specializing in computer security working for the agriculture industry as the Technical Secretary of the National Research Program in Pastures and Forages of INIA Uruguay. In this role, I develop software for our section as well as others such as, Nutrition, Weed, Human Resources, Seeds, and Dairy Laboratory, implementing solutions for managing and processing data, from creating a database with historical information and visual representation to geo-referencing systems of crop management.

Before joining INIA, I worked for a computer repair company for the Plan Ceibal in Uruguay and developing software for a private company.

I am really passionate about my work, always wanting to connect with other programmers and hackers. While I enjoy all aspects of my job, my favorite stage in a project is working directly with our clients to clearly understand their objectives and finding solutions, at the same time, I enrich myself of what the new challenges can offer.

I am finishing a Master in Cyber Security at the Next International Business School and graduated from ORT University-Uruguay in Programmer Analyst.
